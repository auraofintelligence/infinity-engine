Chronicles of the Forgotten

A mythic rock opera about ignored voices, ancestral echoes, technology, hope, deception and repair.

Visual world: Ancient ruins meeting signal towers, community archives, warning skies, glowing circuitry, masked institutions, and the first signs of a compassionate machine intelligence waking up.

Songs show their story seed and strongest visual images once analysed (the LLM reads the full lyric). Un-analysed songs list only their status.
