A Protopian Gambit

The fourth-album build: crisis as mirror, protopia as practice, care ledgers, consent, repair, forms, civic courage, and grounded abundance.

Visual world: Solar storms, public ledgers, repair gold, civic counters, purple mats, community halls, builders at dawn, and comic panels that become video keyframes.

Songs show their story seed and strongest visual images once analysed (the LLM reads the full lyric). Un-analysed songs list only their status.
